Welcome to AI In Our Community

Our project explores how participatory artificial intelligence (AI) can be used to support community health initiatives. We partner with local organizations to co-design AI tools that are accessible, ethical, and useful for underserved populations.

Our Mission

We aim to bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI research and practical community impact, ensuring that technology benefits everyone, especially those traditionally hard to reach.
